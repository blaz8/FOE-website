Our Mission

Front Desk Academy exists to help receptionists around the world become confident, capable, and career-ready. We believe that great guest experiences begin with empowered front desk teams—and we’re here to support that from day one.

Why We Started

Founded by hospitality educator Blaž Zver, the Academy was created to fill the training gap for hotel receptionists. We provide real-world skills, emotional intelligence tools, and guest service techniques that work in today’s fast-paced hospitality environment.

What We Stand For

We value clarity, calmness, and professionalism. Our training is designed to be practical, digestible, and immediately useful—so you can apply what you learn on your next shift. Whether you're a beginner or growing toward leadership, you're welcome here.
